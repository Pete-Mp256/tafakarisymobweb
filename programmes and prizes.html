Our Programmes

The Tafakari Symposium offers a range of programmes aimed at engaging theological scholars, church leaders, and community members in transformative learning experiences.

1. Leadership Development Programme

This programme trains local leaders in informal settlements to engage with theology from a grassroots perspective.

2. Urban Theology & Social Justice

Exploring faith-based responses to social justice, poverty, and urban transformation.

3. Community-Based Theological Education

Providing practical and contextual theological training for pastors and lay leaders.

Prizes & Recognitions

Outstanding contributors to urban theology and education receive recognition and awards.
