Policy & Resources

The Tafakari Symposium is committed to providing essential resources and shaping policies that empower leaders in informal settlements. Our mission is to bridge theological education with real-world impact through accessible knowledge and advocacy.

Key Policy Focus Areas

  • Urban Theology & Social Justice: Policies that address faith-based responses to poverty and marginalization.
  • Inclusive Education: Promoting accessible theological education for grassroots leaders.
  • Community-Driven Change: Encouraging policies that support local leadership in decision-making.

Resource Hub

We provide a growing collection of research papers, case studies, and training materials to support theological education in informal settlements.

Advocacy & Collaboration

We work with churches, NGOs, and academic institutions to develop policies that address urban challenges through faith-based action. Join us in shaping the future of theological education and urban mission.
