Education & Skills Development

At the Tafakari Symposium, we believe that theological education should be dynamic, practical, and accessible to all. Our programs focus on equipping leaders in informal settlements with the skills and knowledge needed for transformational leadership.

Key Areas of Focus

  • Contextual Theology: Understanding faith and ministry within marginalized communities.
  • Leadership Training: Developing leaders who can drive social change in urban settings.
  • Community Engagement: Empowering grassroots leaders through collaborative learning.
  • Research & Reflection: Encouraging theological inquiry rooted in real-world experiences.

Workshops & Learning Sessions

Our symposium includes hands-on workshops led by experts in theology, urban mission, and community transformation. These sessions will explore:

  • Pedagogy from Below: Rethinking theological education for informal settlements.
  • Faith & Justice: The role of the church in advocating for social change.
  • Skills Development: Practical tools for effective leadership and ministry.
