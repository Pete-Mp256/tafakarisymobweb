About Tafakari Symposium

The Tafakari Symposium is a transformative space for dialogue and learning, bringing together theologians, church leaders, and community advocates to explore theological education through the lens of social justice and grassroots leadership.

Our Mission

We seek to equip and empower informal settlement leaders through contextual theological education, fostering a pedagogy that listens, engages, and responds to the realities of marginalized communities.

Our Vision

To create a movement of urban theological educators, practitioners, and faith leaders committed to reshaping theological learning for social transformation.

Our Partners

The symposium is hosted in collaboration with:

  • Center for Urban Mission
  • Local and international theological institutions
  • Community-based organizations
